NO JOB WILL DEFINE ME

NO MATTER WHAT YOU DO

Sometimes I wonder: what if I had chosen agriculture, to be a farmer?

Would that decision have led me down to a different path?  Would dedicating my life to grapes, oranges, or apples have gotten me where I am no matter what?

I worked as a carpenter, electrician and teacher.   Being a teacher almost defined me, but then I got my degree in electrical engineering and computers and,  most likely, followed another path. Afterwards I chosed to create my own business dedicated to solutions for smart buildings.  Apparently I chose electrical engineering and computer science above all else, although that doesn’t define me.  Other apples and lately also some penguins, I believe it is what I do with my knowledge that will define me.

I will always remember that one day I was a carpenter and electrician,… But being a teacher is engraved in my character

Sharing my knowledge is my true destiny and passion. That’s why my motto: "Gentlemen let's broaden our minds".

PS.: Traveling would definitely have to be part of any option.

SEA:ME 

Software engineering in automotive and mobility systems

In Portugal, SEA:ME is settled in Porto, where innovative educators from Shaken not stirred have established a cutting-edge lab dedicated to advancing education in the mobility sector. Collaborating with leading companies in the field, this initiative aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry needs by equipping students with the skills required for the rapidly evolving mobility labor market.

The first cohort of students will embark on this journey in November 2024, engaging with industry experts and working on groundbreaking projects for the future of mobility.

42, ASCII (‘*’), anything

my path, my code, your asset

The 42 program was designed in Paris in 2013 and is constantly developing. Each student progresses by developing projects, earning points to pass the stage, as if it were a game. Dedicating around 40 hours a week to 42, students must complete the program in a period of between 12 and 18 months, after which they are expected to dive into their first professional experience.

Once this phase is complete, students will be able to apply for a 42 degree diploma. It is up to each individual after that to decide whether they want to deepen their knowledge by delving into one of the various areas of specialization available.

The project based and peer to peer methodology, as well as the professional experiences integrated into the curriculum, prioritize practical and team work, preparing students for the future.
